The legend of the dancing squirrels camp .

One hot summer day, on this side of the earth, lived hundreds of squirrels in peace and quiet, until one day when the drought settled over the forest. The squirrels began to worry because they didn't have enough water to survive and they did everything possible. As they were about to give up, a crowd of children appeared, carrying a bright, powerful aura behind them and promising the squirrels that they would save them from the drought.
One evening, the children started to dance a special dance, called the rain dance and suddenly the forest was hit by a strong wind and a big storm, so big that it rained for 5 days and 5 nights. It rained so much that a lake formed around the forest, and the lake has survived to the present day. 
The squirrels were very grateful, and for the good deeds of the children, every night they dance ”the rain dance”.
They were so happy !
Now any child is welcome in their forest, forever.

Where will my children sleep?


Throughout the camp, we have small houses, made for 4 children each.
There are 2 bunk beds, with two large closets and a private bathroom, per house. 

There is a night and day guard who will patrol the camp constantly.

Children are not allowed to leave the houses after closing time.
Every morning a tutor will wake them up.
